Simple Omelette

An easy, savory, fluffy, and delicious omelette for breakfast lovers. This omelette can be filled and topped with anything you like!

For this recipe you will need an oven-safe skillet, a rubber spatula, a food processor, and an oven mitt. Ingredients are as follows:
  • 3/4 cups firm silken tofu, drained and gently patted dry
  • 2 tbsp Hummus
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 cup of veggies of choice
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Heat butter in a small-to-medium, oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Tilt pan to coat bottom
  3. Prep veggies, drain and dry tofu, and mince garlic. Set aside
  4. Heat a small-to-medium, oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add olive oil and minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes or until just lightly golden brown
  5. Transfer garlic to food processor, along with remaining omelet ingredients (tofu – cornstarch) and mix to combine, scraping down sides as needed. Add just a touch of water to thin, 1-2 tbsp at most. Set aside
  6. Add the veggies to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper and sauté to desired doneness
  7. Pour in the omelette batter and cook over medium heat on the stove top for 5 minutes until the edges start to dry
  8. Place skillet in oven and bake until dry and deep golden brown – 10-15 minutes. The longer it bakes the less soft/wet it will be, so if you prefer a more “well done” omelet, cook closer to 15 or more
  9. Carefully remove from oven with oven mitt and fold over gently with a spatula. If it doesn’t want to fold, you can serve it scrambled!
  10. Serve with desired toppings and enjoy!
You should expect a full stomach after enjoying this wholesome omelette!
