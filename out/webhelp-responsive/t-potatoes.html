Best Ever Breakfast Potatoes

These hearty and home style baked breakfast potatoes are so easy and will become a go-to recipe.

Materials for this recipe include a large mixing bowl, a measuring cup, and a baking sheet. Ingredients are as follows:
  • 5 pounds red potatoes, roughly chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and roughly chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 stick Earth Balance Vegan Buttery Stick
  • 1 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 6-8

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes, shaking the pan twice
  3. Raise the heat to 500 degrees and bake until crisp and brown, 10 to 15 minutes
  4. Sprinkle with a little more salt and pepper before serving.
    You will have a filling breakfast side dish that can be enjoyed by a big Sunday brunch crowd or saved for more breakfasts in the future.
Store the leftover potatoes in an airtight container in the fridge. Let other people know they're in there so they don't go to waste!
